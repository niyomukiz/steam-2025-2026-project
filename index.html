About Agahozo-Shalom Youth Village

Agahozo-Shalom Youth Village (ASYV) is a residential community in Rwanda for orphaned and vulnerable youth who lost family in the 1994 genocide against the Tutsi and its aftermath. Founded in 2008, ASYV provides students with a transformative education, emotional support, and practical skills needed to thrive.

The STEAM Exhibition Hub represents our commitment to 21st-century education, where students don't just learn theories but apply knowledge to create real-world solutions. Through hands-on projects, mentorship, and collaborative learning, we're preparing the next generation of innovators.
